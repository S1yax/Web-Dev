Muffins

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 5-6 people
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Baking Powder & Baking Soda 5 г
Butter 120 г
Sugar 200 г
Eggs 2 шт
Salt, Cinnamon, & Vanilla Extract 1 tea spoon
Milk 120 мл

Steps:

1. Sift 220 g of flour with 5 g of baking powder into a bowl (you can sift the flour twice).

2.Place 120 g of softened butter in the bowl of a stand mixer. Beat until the butter turns white. While continuing to beat, add 200 g of sugar. Continue beating for another 1-2 minutes.

3.While whisking, add 2 eggs and 5 g of vanilla sugar one at a time. Beat until the mixture turns white. While whisking, scrape down the sides of the bowl and stir until the dough is smooth

4.Then reduce the mixer speed to minimum and add the flour and 120 ml of milk. Add the flour and milk alternately, in three stages.

5.Using a pastry bag, an electric ice cream scoop, or a regular tablespoon, spoon the batter into the paper cupcake liners (fill them 2/3 full). Bake the cupcakes in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F). Bake for approximately 20 minutes. You can check for doneness with a wooden skewer.

Pizza

  • Time: 85 minutes
  • Serves: 3-5 person
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 150 g
Dry yeast 3 г
Olive oil 1 table spoon
Sugar 0.5 tea spoon
Water 80 ml
Salt 0.5 tea spoon
Cheese 120 g

Steps:

1. Prepare the yeast pizza dough. Pour warm boiled water into a bowl, add sugar, and dry yeast. Stir.

2.Then pour in sunflower oil, or better yet, olive oil. Add salt and stir again.

3.Knead the dough, adding flour in batches. Form the dough into a ball, cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rest in a warm place for 40 minutes.

4. Punch down the risen yeast pizza dough. On a floured sheet of parchment paper, roll the dough into a thin circle approximately 25 cm in diameter.

5. Spread ketchup or any other pizza sauce on the pizza base.Slice the tomato thinly, removing the stem. Arrange the tomato slices on the pizza. Grate the hard cheese on a coarse grater and sprinkle it over the pizza toppings.

6. Bake homemade pizza in a preheated oven at 200 degrees for about 15 minutes.
