Below is the list of places you can go:

  1. Lake Kaindy

    2. Lake Kaindy was formed as a result of an earthquake in 1911. The natural disaster caused a large landslide that blocked the gorge, forming a natural dam. Water from nearby rivers and springs filled the gorge, creating a beautiful lake. A unique feature of the lake is the presence of submerged spruce trees at its bottom, which still stand upright, their tops protruding above the water's surface.

  2. Bektau-Ata

    3. Bektau-Ata is a unique mountain range located 70 kilometers north of the city of Balkhash. This range is a combination of granite cliffs, canyons, and caves, creating breathtaking landscapes and offering excellent opportunities for outdoor activities. The granite peaks of Bektau-Ata offer excellent opportunities for climbing and rock climbing. The mountain range also boasts several caves for exploration, known for their unique shapes and beautiful geological formations.

  3. Kiin-Kerish Canyon

    4. The Kiin-Kerish Canyon is known for its multicolored cliffs of red, orange, and white. Millions of years ago, tectonic processes and erosion formed these cliffs in this beautiful place in Kazakhstan . The canyon is also an important site for paleontological research, as fossils of ancient animals have been found here. Tourists can see various geological formations, such as arches, columns, and cliffs. These formations create unique landscapes and are fascinating to explore. The area offers numerous hiking and trekking trails. These routes allow you to explore the canyon and enjoy its natural beauty.
